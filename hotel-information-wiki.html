Hotel Information Index

Complete Reference Guide for Hotel Data Collection

Comprehensive index of all information categories needed from the hotel

Purpose: This document serves as a comprehensive index of all information categories needed from the hotel. Use this as a reference to collect and document hotel data for website development, marketing materials, operations, and other business purposes.

1. BASIC HOTEL INFORMATION

Property Identity

  • Official hotel/resort name
  • Legal business name
  • Doing business as (DBA) name (if different)
  • Tagline/slogan
  • Property type (resort, eco-lodge, boutique hotel, etc.)
  • Star rating (if applicable)
  • Year established/opened
  • Number of rooms/units
  • Total property size (acres/hectares)

Location Details

  • Street address
  • Island name
  • Nearest town/city
  • Province/region
  • Country
  • Postal code
  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  • Google Maps link
  • Nearest airport (name and code)
  • Distance from airport
  • Nearest major city
  • Distance from major city

Contact Information

  • Primary phone number (local format)
  • Primary phone number (international format)
  • WhatsApp number
  • Primary email address
  • Reservations email
  • General inquiries email
  • Website URL
  • Business hours
  • Time zone

Legal & Business

  • Tax ID/Business registration number
  • Business registration country
  • Business registration date
  • Business type (LLC, Corporation, Partnership, etc.)
  • Ownership structure
