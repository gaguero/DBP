## Technical Notes

### Image Requirements
- **Width**: 800px minimum (cards scale responsively)
- **Height**: 600px (4:3 ratio) or 450px (16:9 ratio)
- **Format**: `.avif` preferred for best compression
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit, professional appearance
