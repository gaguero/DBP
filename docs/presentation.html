Implementation Stack

Railway + Headless Frontend Own the Experience

Hosting & Infrastructure
  • Railway hosts the Next/Remix marketing site, the APIs, and every support service together.
  • Automatic deployments keep pages fast worldwide with image delivery and analytics ready to go.
  • Regular backups and mirrored environments protect the rollout while keeping us independent.
Operational Apps
  • EspoCRM lives on the same Railway account so guest and lead data stays in-house.
  • Invoice agent and automations sit alongside the site, sharing one reliable setup.
  • Single sign-on keeps logins simple while giving partners the right permissions.
Implementation Stack

Capture, Nurture, Convert — One Funnel

Dynamic CTAs
  • Contextual pop-ups + sticky bars triggered by scroll depth, exit intent, geo.
  • Lead routing to EspoCRM with UTMs, device, stay interest.
  • Inline itinerary builder blocks auto-generate tailored offers.
Human + AI Workflow
  • The Crafted Hospitality team online 9am-6pm PT (Sun–Sun) for live engagement.
  • Off-hour automations (WhatsApp, email, chat) answer FAQs + collect booking intent.
  • All touchpoints logged to CRM, ready for follow-up scripts and deal stages.
Communications Layer

3CX PBX — Always-On Guest Communications

Numbers & Routing
  • US number (~$2/mo) + Panama DID (~$9/mo) with sub-$6 per 500 minutes usage.
  • Up to 10 free extensions for reservations, concierge, GM, on-site leads.
  • Click-to-call on site + WhatsApp integration for mobile guests.
CRM Integration
  • Call logs, recordings, and SMS sync into EspoCRM deals.
  • Screen-pop of guest profiles; whisper notes for sales readiness.
  • Future-ready for room extensions or kiosk endpoints.
